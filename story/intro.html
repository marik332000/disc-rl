Welcome to Disc RL

Disc RL takes place in the Tron universe. You are a program trapped in a hostile computer. You need to find an Input/Output Tower so that you can re-establish contact with your User.

Controls

Movement is just like NetHack. Use the hjkl keys to move left, down, up, and right. Use yubn to travel the diagonals. Pressing shift with a direction key will safely move you in that direcion as far as possible.
