Welcome to Disc RL

Disc RL takes place in the Tron universe. You are a program trapped in a hostile, infected computer. You need to find an Input/Output Tower so that you can re-establish contact with your User.

Your game is automatically saved, so it is always safe to close the tab. You can visit this page again later to continue your game.

For the full controls listing, see controls.

Movement is just like NetHack. Use the hjkl keys to move left, down, up, and right. Use yubn to travel the diagonals.

Walking into an enemy performs a standard melee attack. f is used to perform ranged attacks.

Use Q to quit this game and start with a new character.
